### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Full-Stack Developer currently pursuing my **B.Tech in Information Technology**. My engineering philosophy is simple: **Don't just wrap APIs; build the underlying architecture.** Recently coming off an intensive 6-month deep dive into **Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and NLP**, I've shifted my focus from training foundational models in Jupyter Notebooks to deploying them into production environments. I thrive at the intersection of complex data pipelines, vector mathematics, and highly interactive user interfaces.

* 🧠 **My Core Focus:** I specialize in building **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** pipelines, training neural networks (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers), and engineering autonomous AI agents that can actually *do* things rather than just chat.
* 🔭 **What I'm Building:** I am currently developing **OnyxBrowser**, an Electron/FastAPI-based agentic web browser where an integrated AI dynamically interacts with the DOM for complex scraping and automation.
* 🌌 **Design & Architecture:** I love bridging backend physics with frontend aesthetics. Whether it's writing custom GLSL shaders for **ExoLens** or designing futuristic glassmorphism UIs, I draw heavy inspiration from epic sci-fi films and directors like Christopher Nolan and Zack Snyder.
* 🏋️‍♂️ **Beyond the Code:** When I'm not debugging PyTorch tensors or React states, you'll likely find me tracking my macros and hitting the gym for a bodybuilding recomp, analyzing a great action movie, or watching cricket.

### 🚀 Flagship Projects

* 🌐 **OnyxBrowser:** An agentic AI web browser (Electron, FastAPI, LLMs, Web3) capable of autonomous web scraping and DOM manipulation via the "Onyx Intelligence" engine.
* 🛡️ **Spectral Sentinel:** A Deepfake Detection System utilizing PyTorch and CNNs, rigorously trained on the ArtiFact dataset to classify synthetic media.
* 🌌 **ExoLens:** A 3D interactive exoplanet explorer combining a LangChain/ChromaDB RAG pipeline with custom WebGL/GLSL shaders for procedural planetary generation.
